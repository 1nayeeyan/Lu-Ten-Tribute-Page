Lu Ten

Lu Ten at the age of 19 died in a senseless war for his nation. Often known for his jovial and charismatic attitude, he surely will be missed.

Leaves from the vine

Falling so slow

Like fragile tiny shells

Drifting in the foam

Little soldier boy

Come marching home

Brave soldier boy

Comes marching home
